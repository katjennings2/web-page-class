Kat's Christmas Pancakes

These delicious pancakes are sure to get you in the Christmas spirit, year round!

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup of flour
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons of sugar
  • 3 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 3/4 cups of milk
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil or melted butter
  • 1 tablespoon of vanilla extract
  • a dash of cinnamon

Directions

  1. In a medium bowl, beat egg with whisk until fluffy. Stir in remaining ingredients until flour is moistened (batter will be slightly lumpy); do not overmix or pancakes will be tough.
  2. Heat griddle or skillet over medium-high heat (375o). (To test griddle, sprinkles with a few drops of water. If bubbles jump around, heat is just right.) Brush with vegetable oil if necessary (or spray with cooking spray before heating).
  3. For each pancake, pour slightly less than 1/4 cup of batter onto griddle. Cook 2-3 minutes or until bubbly on top and dry around the edges. Turn; cook other side until golden brown.
  4. Add whipped cream and a dash of cinnamon on top. For a festive touch, sprinkled crushed candy cane on top and enjoy!

Prep Time: 15-20 minutes, Cook Time: 15-20 minutes
